Pasta Salad Recipie

Instructions:

This recipie will detail how to make a simple and delicious pasta salad with just a handful of ingredients.

Ingredients:

  • Spiral Pasta
  • Italian style dressing
  • Seasoning of your choice
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Bell pepper
  • Black olives

Steps

  1. Cook and drain pasta
  2. Whisk dressing and seasoning together
  3. Combine pasta and vegetables together
  4. Toss seasoning into pasta vegetable mix
