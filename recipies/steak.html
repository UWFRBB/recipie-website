Marinated Steak Recipie Instructions

Ingredients:

This recipie will detail a delicious marinated flank steak which is both cheap and delicious with this savory marinade.

Ingredients

  • Flank Steak
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Soy Sauce
  • Red Wine
  • Vinegar
  • Lemon Juice
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • Dijon mustrad
  • Garlic
  • Ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Make the marinade
  2. Put steak in fride while in marinade overnight or a minimum of 6 hours
  3. Cook flank steak
  4. Serve steak with your choice of side
