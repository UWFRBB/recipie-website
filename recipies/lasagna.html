Lasagna Recipie Instructions

Instructions:

This recipie will tell you what is needed in order to make a world class lasagna.

The customizable recipie allows for a wide variety of tastes and lets you suit the recipie to your own liking.

Ingredients:

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomota
  • Sugar
  • Seasoning
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make meat sauce
  2. Cook the Noodles
  3. Make ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake it
  6. Let rest for a time then serve
